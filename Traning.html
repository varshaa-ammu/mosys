CAD stands for computer aided design. Developing expertise in CAD enables individuals to utilize computers for 2D and 3D design. CAD is used in a variety of industries and positions, including those in architecture, graphic design, automotive design and more.

CAD (Computer Aided Design) is the use of computer software to design and document a product's design process.
