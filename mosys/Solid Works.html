SolidWorks Standard

Unlock SOLIDWORKS powerful design capabilities and embrace the benefits of the 3D CAD with SOLIDWORKS Standard. Application specific tools allow for rapid creation of parts, assemblies and 2D drawings.

Application specific tools such as sheet metal, weldments, surfacing, and mould tool and die allow for rapid creation of complex parts, assemblies and 2D drawings.

Follow a series of available wizards to aid in the automation of designs and stress analysis as well as monitoring the environmental impact of products and their sustainability.

SOLIDWORKS CAM Standard lets you quickly program individual milled parts and configurations without leaving the SOLIDWORKS 3D CAD environment. SOLIDWORKS CAM Standard is available to current Subscription Services members.

SolidWorks professional

SOLIDWORKS Professional extends Standard's features with file management tools, automated cost estimation as well as photorealistic rendering and a sophisticated components library to name a few.

Standard parts and fasteners libraries alongside automated design and drawing checking allow for increased productivity whilst automated manufacturing estimates can save both time and money.

SolidWorks Premium

Building upon the capabilities of SOLIDWORKS Professional, SOLIDWORKS Premium integrates powerful simulation analysis, design validation, ECAD/MCAD collaboration, reverse engineering, and advanced wire and pipe routing functionality to its list of capabilities.

Advanced simulation capabilities allow users to analyse product performance in real world scenarios, motion and forces. Resolve any potential manufacturability issues prior to production with tolerance stack-up analysis tools which can be used throughout the design process.

Printed circuit board data can be added to your 3D model in a matter of moments with the capability to produce and document layouts for electrical wiring, piping, and tubing. SOLIDWORKS Premium also offers the chance to utilise 3D scanned data within the software.
